About Laym

Who is LaymGlitched?

I'm Laym — an indie game developer who goes by LaymGlitched online. I make games that feel like they have a pulse: weird, atmospheric, and built with intention.

When I'm not in a game engine, I'm documenting the process on YouTube, talking with people in the Discord, and working alongside the team at NanoPaw Studios.
